Biography

I am a second-year bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney (USYD). I received the bachelor's degree from School of Telecommunications Engineering, Xidian University (XDU) in 2020. My research focuses on learning with noisy labels and causal discovery.
